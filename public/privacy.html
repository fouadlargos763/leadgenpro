Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

TL;DR: We collect only what we need to run the service. We never sell your data. You can delete your account anytime.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use LeadGenPro, we collect:

  • Account data: Your name, email address, and password (hashed with bcrypt — never stored in plaintext).
  • Usage data: Lead searches, campaigns created, emails sent, and feature usage — used to enforce plan limits and improve the product.
  • Billing data: Payment is processed by Stripe. We never store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Log data: IP address, browser type, and timestamps for security and abuse prevention.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the LeadGenPro service.
  • To send transactional emails (welcome, receipts, reminders).
  • To enforce plan limits and prevent abuse.
  • To generate anonymised aggregate analytics about platform usage.

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing.
  • Apify — to scrape publicly-available business data.
  • OpenAI — for AI-generated email content (no personal user data is sent to OpenAI).

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. Lead data you have collected is exported to you before deletion upon request.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards including JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, HTTPS enforcement, Content Security Policy headers, and rate limiting on all endpoints.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Export your leads at any time as a CSV file.

7. Contact

For privacy enquiries, please contact us at privacy@leadgenpro.io or via our contact page.
